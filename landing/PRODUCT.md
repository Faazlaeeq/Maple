# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Dentists, clinic owners, and practice managers. They are busy, value efficiency, and are often skeptical of overly complicated tech tools. They want solutions that just work and deliver clear ROI (more patients, fewer missed calls).

## Product Purpose
To sell "Maple", an automated AI receptionist for dental clinics. The goal of this landing page is to convert visitors into subscribers ($20/month) or lifetime users ($300 one-time).

## Brand Personality
Authoritative, calm, clinically precise, but bold and decisive. Not overly playful. Expert confidence.

## Anti-references
- Glowing SaaS tropes (gradients, 3D floating icons, glassmorphism default).
- "Cute" dental tropes (smiling teeth characters, light blue everywhere).
- Aggressive sales-bro marketing.

## Design Principles
- **Show, don't tell**: Let the widget demo itself on the page.
- **Bold commitment**: Strong, decisive use of color and typography. No wishy-washy pastels.
- **Structural precision**: Exacting grids and editorial layout over floaty cards.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict WCAG AA contrast (≥ 4.5:1 for body).
- Legible neo-grotesque and geometric typefaces.
- Reduced motion alternatives for all scroll animations.
